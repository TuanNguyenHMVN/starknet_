What is Starknet Staking?

Starknet is a cutting-edge layer-2 scaling solution for Ethereum, designed to enhance scalability and reduce transaction costs. When you stake your Starknet tokens on our platform, you not only contribute to the network's security but also receive stSTRK tokens.

stSTRK is a liquid staking derivative that represents your staked assets and allows you to remain flexible, trade, or use them in DeFi while still earning staking rewards.
